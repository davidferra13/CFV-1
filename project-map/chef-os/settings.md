# Settings

**What:** 54 configuration pages organized into 6 groups. The most polished surface in the app.

**Route:** `/settings/*`
**Status:** DONE (branding/account/security just built)

## Groups

- **Your Business:** Business defaults, profile & branding, availability rules, booking page, event config, payments & billing
- **Communication:** Workflow settings, notifications & alerts
- **Connections & AI:** Connected accounts, AI & privacy (trust center), client reviews
- **You & Your Career:** Appearance, professional growth, credentials and resume, chef network
- **System & Account:** Legal & protection, sample data, API & developer, desktop app, share feedback, account & security
- **Key pages:** Profile (`/settings/profile`), branding (`/settings/branding`), account (`/settings/account`), credentials (`/settings/credentials`), availability, booking, dashboard widgets, navigation, payments, communication, notifications, integrations, AI trust center, reviews, HACCP compliance, GDPR, embed widget, delete account
