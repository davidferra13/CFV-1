**Version**: 1.0

## CTA Placement

### Primary CTA: "Sign Up"
- **Locations**: Hero section, Final CTA section
- **Link**: `/signup`
- **Styling**: Solid button, high contrast

### Secondary CTA: "Learn More" / "Contact Us"
- **Locations**: Hero section (Learn More), Final CTA (Contact Us)
- **Links**: `/how-it-works`, `/inquire`
- **Styling**: Outline button or text link

---

## CTA Text Rules

- Keep short (2-3 words)
- Action-oriented verbs
- Clear value proposition

**Examples**:
- ✅ "Get Started"
- ✅ "Sign Up Free"
- ❌ "Click here to begin your journey"
