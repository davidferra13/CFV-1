# Public Layer - Pricing Content Rules

**Version**: 1.0
**Date**: 2026-02-14
**Status**: LOCKED

---

## Content Guidelines

### DO
- ✅ Explain pricing philosophy (custom, per-event)
- ✅ Be transparent about payment processing fees
- ✅ Clarify that chefs set their own prices
- ✅ Mention deposit flexibility

### DON'T
- ❌ List specific dollar amounts (prices vary per chef)
- ❌ Promise "free forever" (may change in future)
- ❌ Over-promise features not in V1

---

## Tone
- Professional, transparent
- Avoid marketing hype
- Focus on simplicity
