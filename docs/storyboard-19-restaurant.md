## A-HA MOMENTS (4 total)

1. **Scale:** 5 stations, full staff schedule, daily task board, all managed digitally instead of a whiteboard.
2. **Intelligence:** KDS with allergen flags and aging indicators. Tickets color-coded by urgency.
3. **Speed:** Drag-and-drop station reassignment when someone calls out.
4. **Delight:** Temperature logs with pass/fail indicators. Health inspector-ready documentation.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- This video needs restaurant-specific demo data: daily station assignments, repeating tasks, multiple staff members with shift schedules, KDS tickets, temperature log entries.
- KDS is the visual centerpiece. If tickets don't flow in real-time, pre-seed a set of tickets in various states (new, in progress, fired, served).
- Temperature logs may be a simple form. If the pass/fail threshold logic isn't built, show a manual log with color indicators.
- Staff scheduling with conflict detection is advanced. If conflicts aren't auto-detected, show a manually flagged overlap.
- This video should feel like a busy kitchen tamed by software. Faster pacing than the private chef video.
