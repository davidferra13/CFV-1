# Storyboards #36-45: Social Quick Hits (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)

> 10 vertical videos, 15-30 seconds each, 9:16 format (1080x1920)
> One feature, one hook, one CTA per video.

---

## FORMAT RULES (all 10 videos)

- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (1080x1920)
- **Product fills:** middle 60% of screen
- **Top 20%:** hook text overlay (large, bold, white)
- **Bottom 20%:** caption + CTA
- **Captions:** 28-32px (larger for phone screens)
- **Pacing:** 3-second scenes, hard cuts
- **No fade transitions.** Everything is a hard cut.
- **CTA on last frame:** "cheflowhq.com" or "Link in bio"
- **Music:** 130-140 BPM, uptempo, no lyrics

## PRODUCTION NOTES (all 10)

- Record at 1080x1920 (9:16) or record at 1920x1080 and crop in post.
- Hook text overlay goes in the top 20% of the frame. Use bold white text with text-shadow.
- Captions in the bottom 20%. Larger than YouTube captions (28-32px).
- Hard cuts only. No fades. No transitions. Speed = energy.
- Background music should be the same track across all 10 for brand consistency. Uptempo lo-fi/electronic.
- Record all 10 in one session. They share scenes (dashboard, pipeline, client profile) so batch-record shared screens.
- These can use the same demo data as the YouTube videos. No additional seeding needed.
- Video #45 (brand story) should be the highest production quality of the set. It's the one that gets pinned.
