## PRODUCTION NOTES

- Need a staff account with: 2-3 upcoming assignments, task checklists, time clock data, weekly schedule.
- Staff portal must be functional. If any sections aren't built, focus on what exists and keep the video tight.
- The task checklist with timestamps is the key real-time element. If timestamps aren't auto-generated, show them as pre-seeded.
- Time clock must have a working clock-in button. If not, simulate the state change.
- This video appeals to caterers and restaurants who manage teams. Private chefs rarely have staff portals.
