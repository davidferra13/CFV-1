# Storyboard #1: Take a Chef Integration

> "Take a Chef emails you 50 times a day. What if they all landed in one place?"
> Duration: 60 seconds | Format: 1920x1080 (YouTube 16:9) | Auth: chef account

---

## The pitch in one sentence

Take a Chef floods your inbox with notifications, and you're forced to log into their platform constantly just to keep up. ChefFlow captures every inquiry, message, and booking from those emails automatically. Connect Gmail, and you never have to log into Take a Chef again.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- The Gmail OAuth popup may need to be simulated (fake modal) since Playwright can't do real OAuth in a recording. Alternative: show the settings page AFTER Gmail is already connected, skip the OAuth click, and just show the green "Connected" badge. Caption: "Connect your Gmail. Done."
- Need at least one Take a Chef-sourced inquiry in the agent account's data. If none exist, seed one before recording.
- The Take a Chef payout panel on the event detail Money tab needs an event sourced from Take a Chef. Same seeding requirement.
- Record on beta for fast page loads. Localhost compile delays will destroy the pacing.
- The progress bar should be thin (3px), brand orange gradient, fixed to the top of the viewport.
