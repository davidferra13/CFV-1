# Storyboard #4: What Your Client Sees (Proposal + Payment)

> "Your clients get a white-glove experience. Automatically."
> Duration: 60 seconds | Format: 1920x1080 (YouTube 16:9) | Auth: client account

---

## The pitch in one sentence

Chefs care deeply about looking professional to their clients. This video shows the entire client experience: receiving a proposal, reviewing it, accepting, paying via Stripe, getting an invoice with loyalty points, and seeing their event details. The chef sees what their clients see and thinks "I want my clients to have this experience."

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- This entire video is shot from the CLIENT portal (client auth, not chef). This is intentional: the viewer (a chef) sees their client's experience from the client's perspective. It's like peeking behind the curtain.
- Need a client account with: at least 1 proposal to view, payment history, loyalty tier (Silver or Gold), 2-3 past events.
- The proposal must have a real menu with dietary accommodations visible. Seed an event with guests that have dietary needs (nut allergy, vegan).
- Stripe payment can be simulated with test mode. The Stripe checkout form should look real but use test card numbers. If recording on beta, ensure Stripe is in test mode.
- The loyalty tier should be at least Silver with 1,000+ points. This makes the loyalty program look established and real.
- The client portal UI needs to be polished. Any rough edges in the client experience will be highly visible since the entire video is the client view.
- Record on beta for fast page loads. Client portal pages should be lightweight but the proposal page may have heavier rendering.
