## A-HA MOMENTS (3 total)

1. **Intelligence:** Remy answers business questions from real data. Revenue, client activity, patterns.
2. **Delight:** The suggestion to draft a re-engagement message. Remy is proactive, not just reactive.
3. **Both sides:** Privacy is explicit. Local AI, no cloud. The chef's financial and client data never leaves their machine.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- Remy must be functional and responding accurately. The 3 questions must return real data from the agent account.
- Questions chosen are the most impressive for demos: revenue (concrete number), dormant clients (actionable insight), busiest day (pattern discovery).
- Remy's responses should stream in (character by character) for visual engagement. If streaming isn't working, show the full response appearing.
- The privacy scene is essential. Remy runs on Ollama (local). This is a genuine competitive advantage.
- If Ollama isn't running on beta (Pi), this video must be recorded on localhost where Ollama is available. Alternative: pre-record the Remy responses and show them as if live.
- Keep the questions short and the responses concise. Remy should feel fast and competent, not verbose.
