# ChefFlow Color Variations

Each variation follows the same 6-swatch structure:

1. **Deep** - the richest, darkest expression of the accent
2. **Hero** - the primary interactive color, the one that pops
3. **Soft** - the light highlight, gentle and inviting
4. **Anchor** - the deep near-black with a tint of the accent
5. **Foundation** - the dark gray base (replaces Charcoal Stone)
6. **Text** - the light readable gray (replaces Warm Ash)

## Implementation Notes

- Each variation swaps the 6 identity colors only
- Functional/semantic colors (red for errors, green for success, etc.) stay the same across all variations
- The foundation gray in each variation has a subtle tint of the accent color (not pure neutral gray)
- The text gray is similarly tinted to feel cohesive
- The anchor color is the "darkest brand" used for selection highlights, today markers, deep backgrounds
