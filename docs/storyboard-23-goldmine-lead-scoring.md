## A-HA MOMENTS (3 total)

1. **Intelligence:** Score appears instantly with a full breakdown. No AI, no black box, pure math.
2. **Speed:** High-score leads get priority response. Follow-up deadlines set automatically.
3. **Money:** The 92-score lead is a $5,000 anniversary dinner. The 23-score lead is vague and unqualified. Don't waste time on the wrong ones.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- Need at least 8 inquiries with diverse scores (high, medium, low) and varied data quality.
- The scoring breakdown must show individual component contributions. This is the visual centerpiece.
- The high/low comparison (Scenes 3 and 4) is the "a-ha" moment. The contrast must be stark.
