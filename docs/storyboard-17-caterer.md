## A-HA MOMENTS (4 total)

1. **Scale:** 200 guests, 5 stations, 8 staff, all managed in one system.
2. **Intelligence:** KDS with allergen flags prevents dangerous mistakes during high-volume service.
3. **Speed:** Close-out wizard handles post-event admin for a complex event in 3 minutes.
4. **Delight:** Task board with drag-and-drop and real-time updates keeps the team coordinated.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- This video needs a large-scale event seeded in demo data: 200 guests, 5 stations, 8 staff members, extensive task list, full packing list.
- KDS (Kitchen Display System) must be functional enough to show fire/plate/serve flow. If not fully built, show the UI with pre-set tickets.
- Station management with staff and equipment assignments is the differentiating feature for caterers. If the station UI isn't rich, keep the zoom tight to show what exists.
- Time tracking display with clock-in/out records needs staff data. Seed 8 staff members with time entries.
- The pacing must feel energetic and slightly frenetic: this is a big event, the video should feel like organized chaos being tamed by the system.
