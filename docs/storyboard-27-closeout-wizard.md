## A-HA MOMENTS (3 total)

1. **Speed:** 5 steps, 2 minutes. What used to take 45 minutes of spreadsheet work.
2. **Money:** Mileage auto-calculated as a tax deduction. Most chefs don't track this and lose money.
3. **Intelligence:** Reflection notes are searchable. "What did I learn from the last Henderson dinner?" is a query away.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- The wizard must be functional end-to-end. All 5 steps must work: tip entry, receipt upload (or simulated), mileage, text notes, final close.
- Mileage deduction calculation ($0.67/mile for 2026 IRS rate) should auto-calculate. If not built, show it as a manual display.
- The pacing should feel fast and satisfying. Each step takes 3-5 seconds. The whole wizard is done in 22 seconds of video time.
- Receipt upload can be simulated: drag a pre-prepared file, or show thumbnails appearing after a click.
