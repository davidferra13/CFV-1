# Demo Video Priority Hierarchy

> Ordered by business impact. Make these first.

---

## TIER 1: CONVERSION KILLERS (make these before anything else)

These are the videos that turn a skeptic into a signup. Each one answers "why should I care?"

### 1. Take a Chef / Private Chef Manager Integration

- **Why first:** 80% of private chefs use TakeAChef. Show them ChefFlow auto-captures every inquiry, message, and booking from their Gmail. No more checking TakeAChef 50 times a day. This alone gets signups.
- **The hook:** "Stop checking TakeAChef. It checks itself."
- **Show:** Gmail sync setup (3 clicks) > new TakeAChef inquiry appears automatically > lead score assigned > client record created > dashboard widget showing TAC stats
- **Auth:** chef
- **Duration:** 60s

### 2. Full Lifecycle: Inquiry to Invoice

- **Why:** The "holy shit this does everything" video. One flow, 90 seconds, the whole platform.
- **The hook:** "From first message to final payment. One platform."
- **Show:** Inquiry with lead score > create event > build quote > send proposal > client accepts > record payment > invoice > done
- **Auth:** chef
- **Duration:** 90s

### 3. The Command Center (Dashboard)

- **Why:** First thing a chef sees after signup. If this looks powerful, they stay.
- **The hook:** "Open your laptop. Know exactly what to do."
- **Show:** Priority queue, week strip, revenue snapshot, health score, upcoming events
- **Auth:** chef
- **Duration:** 60s

### 4. What Your Client Sees (Proposal + Payment)

- **Why:** Chefs care about looking professional to their clients. This shows the client experience is premium.
- **The hook:** "Your clients get a white-glove experience. Automatically."
- **Show:** Client receives proposal > reviews it > accepts > pays via Stripe > gets invoice with loyalty points
- **Auth:** client
- **Duration:** 60s

### 5. The Embeddable Widget

- **Why:** Instant "I need this" for any chef with a website. One script tag = inquiry pipeline.
- **The hook:** "Add one line of code. Never miss a lead again."
- **Show:** Settings > embed config > copy script > paste on website > client submits inquiry > it appears in pipeline with lead score
- **Auth:** chef
- **Duration:** 45s

## TIER 2: ARCHETYPE HOOKS (one video per chef type)

Each video speaks directly to one type of chef. "This was built for YOU."

### 6. Private Chef: Your Entire Business in One Place

- **Why:** Core audience. In-home dining, solo operator, juggles everything.
- **The hook:** "You're a chef, not an admin. Act like it."
- **Show:** Inquiry pipeline > event lifecycle > client profiles with dietary/allergies > calendar > food cost > close-out wizard
- **Focus:** Pipeline, Events, Clients, Culinary
- **Auth:** chef
- **Duration:** 90s

### 7. Caterer: Stations, Staff, and Scale

- **Why:** Caterers need staff management, station assignments, task boards. Show them ChefFlow handles teams.
- **The hook:** "Run a 200-person event without losing your mind."
- **Show:** Event with staff assignments > station setup > task board > packing list > KDS > time tracking > close-out
- **Focus:** Staff, Tasks, Stations, Events
- **Auth:** chef
- **Duration:** 90s

### 8. Meal Prep Chef: Subscriptions and Repeat Clients

- **Why:** Meal preppers live on repeat clients. Loyalty, dietary tracking, and scheduling matter most.
- **The hook:** "Your clients come back every week. Your tools should know that."
- **Show:** Client with dietary profile > recurring events > loyalty tiers > calendar with prep blocks > recipe costing
- **Focus:** Clients, Culinary, Calendar, Loyalty
- **Auth:** chef
- **Duration:** 75s

### 9. Restaurant / Cafe Owner: Daily Ops

- **The hook:** "Your kitchen runs itself. Almost."

### 10. Food Truck: Mobile and Fast

- **Why:** Food truckers need mobile-first, travel planning, high-volume.

- **Focus:** Mobile, Travel, Tasks, Calendar

- **Duration:** 60s

### 11. Bakery / Pastry: Orders and Production

- **Why:** Order-driven, production scheduling, recipe costing with precision.
- **The hook:** "Every gram matters. So does every dollar."
- **Show:** Recipe with precise costing > food cost breakdown > inquiry pipeline for custom orders > client preferences
- **Focus:** Culinary, Pipeline, Costing, Clients
- **Auth:** chef
- **Duration:** 60s

### 12. Grazing Table Specialist

- **Why:** Growing niche. Board building, vendor sourcing, visual presentation, event packets.
