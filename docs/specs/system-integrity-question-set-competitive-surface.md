# System Integrity Question Set: Competitive Surface & Market Readiness

> **Purpose:** Expose every failure point where ChefFlow's built system diverges from what the market actually demands. Every question is verifiable against code, data, or observable behavior. Answers force the system into a fully specified state.
>
> **Benchmark:** 120 platforms across 6 categories (restaurant booking, food experiences, private chef hiring, catering/events, meal delivery, general booking). Research date: 2026-04-18.
>
> **Scoring:** PASS = verified working with evidence. PARTIAL = exists but incomplete. FAIL = missing or broken. N/A = intentionally out of scope with documented rationale.
>
> **Audit date:** 2026-04-18. All verdicts verified against codebase.

---
