## Domain 1: Intake Lane Parity (Open Booking vs Direct Inquiry)

The `/book` (open booking) and `/chef/[slug]/inquire` (direct inquiry) are the two primary consumer-to-chef pipelines. They share the same database tables but diverge in data richness, notification depth, and post-submission automation. Every question here forces specification of whether parity is intentional or accidental.
