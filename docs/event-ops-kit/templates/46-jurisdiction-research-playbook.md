# Jurisdiction Research Playbook

## Goal

For each service area, confirm legal, health, and event-operation requirements before selling events.

## Research Sequence

1. Identify target city/county/state.
2. Confirm business license requirements.
3. Confirm food/catering permit requirements.
4. Confirm commissary or commercial kitchen requirements.
5. Confirm temporary event permit requirements.
6. Confirm food handler/manager certification requirements.
7. Confirm insurance/COI expectations common in local venues.

## Verification Rules

- Use primary government sources first.
- Save agency pages and contact details in your research sheet.
- Record "last verified" date for every row.
- Re-verify before each major event season.

## Output

Maintain all findings in `spreadsheets/jurisdiction-research-starter.csv`.
