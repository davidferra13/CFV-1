# Allergy Disclosure Language Set

## Proposal/Contract Language

"Client must disclose all food allergies and dietary restrictions in writing by the final menu deadline. We use reasonable controls to reduce cross-contact risk but cannot guarantee a fully allergen-free environment unless explicitly contracted and operationally feasible."

## Label Footer Language

"Contains or may contain: [insert allergens]. Ask staff before consuming if you have food allergies."

## Team Response Script

"I will confirm the ingredient list with our lead before answering. We do not guess on allergens."

## Website/Inquiry Language

"Please share all allergies and dietary restrictions at inquiry and again before final headcount. Last-minute changes may be limited by sourcing and prep timelines."
