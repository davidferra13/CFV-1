# Monthly P&L Review Guide

Use with: `spreadsheets/monthly-pl-review.csv`

## Monthly Review Steps

1. Enter total events and total revenue for the month.
2. Enter actual food, labor, rental/disposable, transport, and overhead costs.
3. Confirm total cost and gross profit.
4. Calculate gross margin percent.
5. Add notes on major variance drivers.

## Required Follow-Ups

- If gross margin is below target, identify top 3 causes.
- Adjust package pricing or minimums where needed.
- Review high-variance vendors and product swaps.
- Review labor scheduling efficiency.
