# Business Setup Checklist

## Entity and Tax

- [ ] Business entity formed
- [ ] EIN obtained
- [ ] State tax registration completed
- [ ] Sales tax setup completed (if required)

## Banking and Accounting

- [ ] Business bank account opened
- [ ] Accounting software configured
- [ ] Chart of accounts configured
- [ ] Receipt and expense capture workflow set

## Licensing and Permits

- [ ] Business license active
- [ ] Catering/food permit active
- [ ] Commissary agreement signed and filed

## Insurance

- [ ] General liability active
- [ ] Product liability active
- [ ] Auto/hired non-owned auto active
- [ ] Workers' comp (if required) active

## Legal Documents

- [ ] Service agreement finalized
- [ ] Cancellation/refund policy finalized
- [ ] Allergy disclosure language finalized
- [ ] Damage/loss policy finalized
