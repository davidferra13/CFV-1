# Discovery Call Script

## Call Goals

- Confirm fit.
- Confirm budget and scope.
- Capture constraints early.
- Advance to proposal.

## Script

1. "Tell me about your event vision and guest experience goals."
2. "What is your expected guest count and final count deadline?"
3. "Do you already have a venue, and what is the load-in/setup window?"
4. "Any dietary restrictions or allergy concerns we need to design around?"
5. "Which service style do you want: drop-off, styled setup, or staffed?"
6. "What budget range are you targeting?"
7. "Would you like decor/floral handled by us as part of setup?"
8. "What is your decision timeline?"

## Close

"I will send your proposal by [date]. Once approved, your date is secured with signed agreement and deposit."
