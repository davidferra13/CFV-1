# Role Cards by Event Size

## Small Event (up to 60 guests)

- Lead stylist (1): setup plan, final QA, client handoff
- Prep/support (1): food staging, labeling, tool setup

## Medium Event (61-150 guests)

- Lead stylist (1)
- Food lead (1)
- Setup assistants (1-2)
- Runner/logistics (1)

## Large Event (151+ guests)

- Event lead (1)
- Styling lead (1)
- Food lead (1)
- Setup assistants (2-4)
- Runner/logistics (1-2)
- Service attendants (as required by service model)

## Core Expectations (All Roles)

- Arrive at call time
- Follow food safety and allergen SOPs
- Use run sheet and checklist discipline
- Report issues immediately to lead
