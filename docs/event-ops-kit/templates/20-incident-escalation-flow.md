# Incident Escalation Flow

## Trigger

Any food safety issue, allergen concern, injury, or major logistics disruption.

## Step 1: Stabilize

- Remove affected item/service area.
- Stop service in impacted zone.
- Provide immediate first aid/emergency support if needed.

## Step 2: Notify

- Notify team lead immediately.
- Notify venue point of contact.
- Notify client contact when appropriate.
- Call emergency services for medical emergencies.

## Step 3: Document

- Complete incident report in real time if possible.
- Record items, time, location, and witnesses.
- Record corrective actions.

## Step 4: Contain and Correct

- Replace or discontinue affected item.
- Re-sanitize affected area/tools.
- Re-brief team.

## Step 5: Post-Event Review

- Root cause analysis.
- SOP or training updates.
- Formal follow-up communication as needed.
