# Leftovers and Donation Policy

## Default Rule

Ownership of leftovers is defined in the signed service agreement.

## Handling Options

- Client keeps leftovers.
- Venue keeps leftovers.
- Provider removes and disposes.
- Approved donation partner pickup.

## Safety Conditions

- Only items maintained within safe time/temperature controls are eligible for retention or donation.
- Items outside safe limits are discarded.
- Client and venue are notified if discard is required.

## Donation Procedure

1. Confirm recipient organization acceptance policy.
2. Record handoff time and item list.
3. Obtain recipient signature.
4. Store handoff record in event folder.

## Liability Notice

Donation and leftover handling language must match local law and signed contract terms.
