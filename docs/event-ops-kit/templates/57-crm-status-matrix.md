# CRM Status Matrix

| Stage              | Entry Criteria                           | Exit Criteria                | Owner       | SLA                    |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ----------- | ---------------------- |
| New Inquiry        | Inquiry form received                    | Lead qualified or declined   | Sales       | 4 business hours       |
| Qualified          | Date, scope, and budget viable           | Discovery call complete      | Sales       | 2 business days        |
| Discovery Complete | Call completed and requirements captured | Proposal sent                | Sales       | 48 hours               |
| Proposal Sent      | Proposal delivered                       | Booked, declined, or dormant | Sales       | Follow-up cadence      |
| Booked             | Agreement signed + deposit received      | Moved to production          | Sales/Ops   | Same day               |
| Production         | Event planning and execution in progress | Event closed out             | Ops         | Per timeline           |
| Closed Won         | Event completed and reconciled           | Annual review only           | Ops/Finance | N/A                    |
| Closed Lost        | Declined or expired                      | Re-engage if reactivated     | Sales       | Quarterly reactivation |
