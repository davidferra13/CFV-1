## A-HA MOMENTS (3 total)

1. **Money:** Cost per plate calculated from real ingredients. Most chefs have never seen this number.
2. **Intelligence:** Price comparison across stores finds savings the chef didn't know existed.
3. **Scale:** Trend chart shows food cost % over time. The chef spots bad-margin events and fixes the recipe.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- Recipes must have ingredient costs populated. Seed at least 3 recipes with full costing.
- Price comparison requires API data (Kroger, Spoonacular). If APIs aren't returning data, show pre-seeded comparison data.
- Food cost trend chart needs 10+ events with food cost data. Seed events with varying food costs.
- The "38% worst event" highlight is the emotional punch. Make sure one seeded event has a deliberately high food cost.
