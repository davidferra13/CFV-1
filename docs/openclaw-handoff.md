# ChefFlow - OpenClaw Handoff

---

## What It Is

ChefFlow is a business operating platform for culinary professionals. It serves private chefs, caterers, meal prep services, food truck operators, restaurants, and bakeries. It has two sides: one for operators to run their entire business, and one for their clients to manage their experience. There is also a public directory where anyone can find a food service provider near them.

**Live at:** app.cheflowhq.com
**Built on:** Next.js and Stripe

---

## Why It Exists

A chef running their own business spends as much time on admin as they do cooking. Quotes, invoices, client emails, scheduling, grocery costing, staff coordination, contracts, taxes. All of it done across a dozen disconnected tools that do not talk to each other.

ChefFlow puts all of that in one place, automates what can be automated, and always tells the chef what the most important thing to do next is. The cooking is the art. Everything else is ops. ChefFlow handles the ops.

---

## Privacy as a Core Principle

ChefFlow is the only platform in this space where all AI processing stays on the chef's own machine. No client data, dietary information, financial records, or conversations ever touch a cloud AI service. The AI assistant (Remy) runs entirely on local hardware via Ollama. This is not a setting or a toggle. It is how the product is built.

## What Chefs Can Do

Chefs use ChefFlow to run every part of their business from one place.

**Bookings and Events**

- Create and manage events from first inquiry through completion
- Track event status (draft, proposed, accepted, paid, confirmed, in progress, completed, cancelled)
- Build day-of-plans, packing lists, and prep timelines
- Log what went right and wrong after every event
- Close out events with a step-by-step wizard

**Clients**

- Maintain a full client database with contact info, dietary restrictions, allergies, kitchen details, access codes, family notes, and preferences
- Track every interaction, message, and event per client
- See how profitable each client relationship is
- Set reminders for birthdays, anniversaries, and follow-ups
- Build client segments and run targeted outreach

**Money**

- Send quotes and invoices
- Accept credit card payments, Apple Pay, and Google Pay via Stripe
- Track every expense and receipt (with OCR auto-extraction)
- See real profit margins, food cost percentages, and effective hourly rate per event
- Log mileage, tips, and payroll
- Generate tax-ready financial reports
- Set revenue goals and track progress

**Menus and Recipes**

- Build and store menus with courses and dishes
- Maintain a full recipe book (recipes are always entered manually by the chef, never generated by AI)
- Calculate exact food cost per recipe and per event
- Compare grocery prices across multiple vendors in real time
- Track ingredient inventory and flag unused stock for carry-forward

**Staff**

- Add staff members and assign them to events
- Manage daily task boards across the whole team
- Assign tasks to specific kitchen stations
- View real-time staff activity
- Run shift handoffs with notes that carry across days
- Brief staff with AI-generated event briefings

**Scheduling**

- Full calendar with monthly, weekly, and daily views
- Set availability rules, event limits, and buffer days between bookings
- Sync with Google Calendar or export an iCal feed
- See scheduling gaps and burnout warnings

**Communications**

- Message clients by email or SMS from inside the platform
- Use pre-built response templates with variable placeholders
- Set up automated responses and business hours
- Sync Gmail to surface past client conversations
- Run outreach campaigns to client segments

**Analytics**

- 24 business intelligence engines covering revenue trends, booking patterns, client concentration risk, inquiry conversion, price elasticity, churn risk, capacity limits, and more
- All calculated from real data, no AI required

**Operations**

- Morning briefing summarizing yesterday, today's events, active tasks, and alerts
- Daily ops plan with swim lanes (Admin, Prep, Creative, Relationship)
- Priority queue that always surfaces the single most important next action
- Equipment inventory and maintenance tracking
- Travel leg planning and sourcing logistics
- Allergen conflict detection that cross-checks every guest against the event menu (no AI, runs automatically)

**Safety and Legal**

- Auto-generated HACCP food safety plan
- Contract templates and AI-assisted contract drafting
- NDA tracking per client
- GDPR and data privacy controls
- Emergency contact storage

**Growth**

- Public chef profile with reviews, menus, and contact form
- Loyalty program with tiers, points, and rewards
- Collect testimonials and aggregate reviews from Google and Yelp
- Share recipes with other chefs and collaborate on events
- Embeddable inquiry widget for any external website
- Chef network directory for referrals and collaboration

**AI (Remy)**

- Conversational AI assistant available on every page
- Answers questions about clients, events, finances, and recipes in plain language
- Generates daily plans, drafts messages, and surfaces insights
- All AI runs locally on the chef's hardware via Ollama. No client data ever leaves the machine.

## What Clients Can Do

Clients get their own dedicated portal. They log in and see only their own events and relationship with their chef.

**Managing Their Booking**

- View all current and past events with full details
- Review and accept a proposal
- Browse and choose a menu, or submit personal preferences (cuisine, loves, hates, dining style)
- Approve or request revisions to a proposed menu
- Sign the service contract digitally
- View a countdown page with parking, dress code, and arrival instructions

**Payments**

- Pay deposits and full balances online via Stripe
- View and download their invoice as a PDF
- See a payment plan if installments were set up
- Redeem gift cards or vouchers at checkout

**Guest Management**

- Share the event with guests via a link
- Track RSVPs
- Submit dietary restrictions and allergies for the whole guest list

**Before the Event**

- Complete a pre-event checklist (dietary confirmation, kitchen details, logistics)
- Receive reminders from their chef

**Communication**

- Message their chef directly through the portal
- View the full conversation history

**After the Event**

- Complete a post-event satisfaction survey
- Leave a review
- View an event summary and receipt

**Loyalty and Rewards**

- Track loyalty points and tier status (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum)
- See progress toward the next tier
- Redeem rewards
- View gift cards and vouchers

**Account and History**

- Edit their profile, preferences, and contact info
- View complete spending history across all events
- See all past quotes and their status
- Track all inquiries from initial contact to booked event

**Dinner Circle (Social)**

- Create private dinner planning groups with friends
- Invite friends to a circle
- Share chef recommendations with friends
- Receive notifications from the circle

**Booking**

- Submit a new booking request directly to their chef from inside the portal

## The Public Directory (No Login Required)

Anyone can visit ChefFlow and search for private chefs, caterers, food trucks, meal prep services, restaurants, and bakeries near them. They can view real menus, read reviews, and send an inquiry directly to the provider. No commission is taken. The platform connects people with food, it does not handle the transaction.

---

## Public Booking Page (No Login Required)

Every chef can have a public booking page at their own URL. Visitors see the chef's headline, availability calendar, and pricing, then submit a booking request or instantly book depending on how the chef has set it up. No account needed to book.

---

## Partner Portal (Venue and Location Partners)

Venue owners and location partners get their own login. They can see how many events have been hosted at their space, how many guests were served, view photos from those events, manage their location listings, and see their partnership origin story. They do not see any financial data.

---

## Staff Portal (Staff Login Required)

Staff members and contractors get their own login. They see only what they need: their assigned tasks for the day, which kitchen station they are working, the day-of plan for events they are assigned to, and the ability to mark tasks complete. They have no access to client data, financial data, or business settings.

---

## Kiosk and Point of Sale

ChefFlow has a kiosk mode for in-person transactions, such as at food trucks, pop-ups, or on-site at events. It runs as a full-screen interface for browsing items and processing payments via Stripe.

---

## In One Sentence

ChefFlow is the operating system for culinary professionals: chefs run their entire business on it, their clients manage their entire experience on it, and anyone looking for a food experience can find one through it.
