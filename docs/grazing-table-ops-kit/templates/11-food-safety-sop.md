# Food Safety SOP (Template)

## Purpose

Maintain safe food handling across receiving, prep, holding, transport, service, and cleanup.

## Scope

Applies to all staff and all events.

## Core Controls

- Keep cold items at or below 41 F during holding/transport unless approved time control is used.
- Track time and temperature in written logs.
- Discard any item outside policy limits.
- Use calibrated probe thermometers.
- Enforce handwashing and glove rules.

## Receiving

1. Check delivery temperature and condition.
2. Reject damaged or temperature-abused product.
3. Label product with receive date and use-by date.

## Prep

1. Wash/sanitize prep surfaces before and after use.
2. Use separate tools/boards for raw and ready-to-eat items.
3. Keep batches small to reduce time out of temperature control.

## Holding and Transport

1. Pre-chill coolers and packs before loading.
2. Keep lids closed except during active use.
3. Log temperatures at dispatch, arrival, and setup completion.

## Service Window

1. Follow defined display-time rule.
2. Record service start time and mandatory pull/discard time.
3. Replace utensils if contaminated or dropped.

## Cleaning and Sanitizing

1. Clean surfaces to remove soil.
2. Sanitize with approved food-safe solution.
3. Air-dry surfaces and tools.
4. Log sanitizer concentration checks.

## Corrective Actions

- If temperatures exceed limits, move items to safe control immediately.
- If safe recovery is not possible, discard item and document.
- Notify team lead and complete incident/corrective action log.

## Training

- New staff must complete food safety orientation.
- Team lead confirms pre-event safety briefing at each event.
