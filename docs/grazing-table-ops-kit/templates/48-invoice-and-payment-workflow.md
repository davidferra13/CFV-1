# Invoice and Payment Workflow

## Trigger Points

- Booking deposit invoice at contract signature
- Interim invoice (if used) at midpoint milestone
- Final invoice at final-count lock or pre-event due date

## Invoice Checklist

- [ ] Correct event reference
- [ ] Correct package and scope
- [ ] Correct tax and fees
- [ ] Payment due date clear
- [ ] Payment links included

## Reminder Cadence

- Reminder 1: 7 days before due date
- Reminder 2: 2 days before due date
- Reminder 3: day after missed due date

## Escalation

- If unpaid by escalation threshold, pause non-essential services and notify client per contract terms.
