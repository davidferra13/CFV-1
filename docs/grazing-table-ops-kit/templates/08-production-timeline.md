# Production Timeline Template

## T-7 to T-5 Days

- Confirm final proposal and event details.
- Confirm vendor availability for all core ingredients.
- Confirm rentals and decor inventory.
- Confirm staffing assignments and call times.

## T-4 to T-3 Days

- Place ingredient orders.
- Prep dry goods and shelf-stable elements.
- Print labels and allergen cards draft.
- Stage equipment bins.

## T-2 Days

- Receive perishables and inspect quality.
- Begin prep for spreads, garnishes, and non-fragile components.
- Update substitutions if supplier changes occur.
- Confirm route and load plan.

## T-1 Day

- Cut/portion cheeses and meats where quality allows.
- Prep fruits and vegetables according to hold rules.
- Assemble boards/trays if service model permits.
- Wrap and store according to cold-chain SOP.
- Final staffing and weather check.

## Event Day (T-0)

- Final temperature check at dispatch.
- Load vehicles by fragility and unload sequence.
- Transport with minimal disturbance.
- Assemble/stage at venue.
- Complete final QA and handoff.

## T+1 Day

- Retrieve rentals/equipment.
- Sanitize and reset inventory.
- Complete closeout and margin review.
