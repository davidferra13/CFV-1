# Booking-to-Event Communication Timeline

## At Booking (T-30 to T-90)

- Send booking confirmation
- Send payment schedule summary
- Send intake and venue questionnaire links

## T-21

- Confirm preliminary menu direction
- Confirm decor/floral direction
- Confirm venue access assumptions

## T-14

- Confirm staffing model
- Confirm rentals and equipment requirements
- Send reminder for dietary/allergen final deadline

## T-7

- Send logistics confirmation message
- Confirm load-in details and contact chain
- Confirm weather contingency messaging if needed

## T-3

- Send final prep underway update
- Confirm arrival window
- Confirm final payment status

## T-1

- Send final execution confirmation
- Share day-of lead contact

## Event Day

- Send setup complete confirmation
- Send any major live updates if relevant

## T+1 to T+3

- Send thank-you and review request
- Send photo recap timeline
- Confirm leftover/equipment closeout completion
