## 1. Warm Direct Outreach

Use for:

- past clients willing to be candid
- friends or contacts who host dinners or events
- planners, venues, or referrers
- direct email, text, or DM outreach

Template:

```text
I'm collecting honest input from people who have hired, seriously considered, or are curious about hiring a private chef or similar food service for a personal event. This survey is anonymous, takes about 4-6 minutes, and is specifically about discovery, booking, pricing, and what makes the process feel easy or difficult. If you're open to it, I'd really value your input: [SURVEY LINK]
```

Slightly warmer version:

```text
I'm trying to learn what actually makes hiring a chef feel easy, trustworthy, and worth booking. This survey is anonymous, takes about 4-6 minutes, and would be genuinely helpful if you've ever hired one, considered it, or thought about it: [SURVEY LINK]
```

---

## Messaging Rules

1. Lead with the respondent's experience, not the product.
2. Keep the ask short.
3. Mention anonymous and `4-6 minutes`.
4. Focus on discovery, trust, pricing clarity, booking friction, and planning experience.
5. Do not promise a finished product.
6. Do not overuse feature names.
7. Do not reuse the same copy everywhere without tracking where it was used.
