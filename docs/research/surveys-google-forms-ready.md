# SURVEY 1: FOOD OPERATOR SURVEY

## Form Settings

- **Title:** How Do You Run Your Food Business?
- **Description:** A quick survey for independent food operators (private chefs, caterers, meal prep, grazing boards, food trucks). Your answers directly shape a tool being built for people like you. Takes 8-12 minutes. All responses are anonymous.

**Section title:** Who You Are
**Section description:** A few quick questions about your business.
