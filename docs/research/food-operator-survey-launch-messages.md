## 1. Warm Direct Outreach

Use for:

- people you already know
- previous operator contacts
- chef/operator DMs
- direct email or text outreach

Template:

```text
I'm working on a tool for real food operators and need honest input from people actually doing the work. This survey is anonymous, takes about 5-7 minutes, and is specifically about pricing, workflow, sourcing, and day-to-day operations. If you're open to it, I'd really value your response: [SURVEY LINK]
```

Slightly warmer version:

```text
I'm getting direct input from real food operators before building further. The survey is anonymous, takes about 5-7 minutes, and focuses on pricing, workflow, sourcing, and the operational side of running the business. If you're willing, I'd really value your perspective: [SURVEY LINK]
```

Template:

```text

```

---

## Messaging Rules

1. Lead with the operator's reality, not the product.
2. Keep the ask short.
3. Mention anonymous and `5-7 minutes`.
4. Focus on pricing, workflow, sourcing, margin, and operations.
5. Do not promise a finished product.
6. Do not overuse feature names.
7. Do not post the same copy everywhere without tracking where it was used.
