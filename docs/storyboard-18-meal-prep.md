## A-HA MOMENTS (4 total)

1. **Intelligence:** 24 weeks of client preferences saved. No repeat meals. The system remembers what the chef might forget.
2. **Money:** Cost per serving with real ingredient prices. 68% margin visible. Scale the recipe, costs update.
3. **Scale:** Recurring calendar events with client breakdown. One prep day, three clients, 15 meals.
4. **Delight:** Consolidated grocery list combines all clients' needs. Buy once, prep for everyone.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- Meal prep chefs are a specific niche. This video should feel fast, efficient, volume-oriented. Smaller dollar amounts but higher frequency.
- Seed demo data with: 3+ recurring clients with diverse dietary needs, 24+ past events for one client, recipes with ingredient costing, recurring calendar events.
- The consolidated grocery list is a powerful feature for meal preppers. If it's not built as a dedicated view, show the individual event packing lists and manually note that they could be combined.
- Recurring events on the calendar are essential. If recurring events aren't built, show weekly blocks that look recurring.
- Loyalty tier perks specific to meal prep (free add-on every 10th order) should match what's configured in the loyalty system.
