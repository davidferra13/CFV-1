## 💡 Key Pain Points & Goals

**Pain Points:**

1. **Inventory Blind Spots:** Difficulty knowing exactly what stock is available across multiple storage areas in real-time.
2. **Waste Tracking:** Manual logging of spoilage and over-ordering leads to significant financial waste.
3. **Labor Inefficiency:** Time spent searching for ingredients or manually updating spreadsheets slows down prep work.
4. **Recipe Scaling Errors:** Difficulty adjusting recipes accurately when ingredient availability changes or when scaling for large events.

**Goals:**

1. **Real-Time Visibility:** Achieve a single, accurate source of truth for all inventory levels.
2. **Waste Reduction:** Implement automated alerts and tracking to minimize spoilage and over-ordering.
3. **Streamlined Workflow:** Digitize the entire prep-to-service process, from ordering to usage.
4. **Cost Control:** Directly link ingredient usage to sales data to improve profitability margins.

---

## 🛠️ Feature Recommendations (The Solution)

Based on the pain points, the system needs to be a comprehensive **Inventory Management System (IMS)** integrated with **Recipe Management**.

### 1. Core Inventory Module (The "What We Have")

- **Barcode/QR Scanning:** Ability to scan incoming goods and outgoing usage items instantly.
- **Multi-Location Tracking:** Track stock levels across walk-in coolers, dry storage, and prep stations.
- **Par Level Alerts:** Automated alerts when stock drops below a predefined minimum threshold (Par Level).
- **Supplier Integration:** Digital catalog of approved suppliers and automated purchase order (PO) generation based on low stock alerts.

### 2. Recipe & Usage Module (The "How Much We Use")

- **Digital Recipe Cards:** Store standardized recipes with precise ingredient measurements.
- **Yield Calculation:** Automatically calculate the total ingredient requirement for a set number of servings/meals.
- **Usage Deduction:** When a dish is "built" or "served," the system automatically deducts the exact ingredients used from the inventory count.
- **Recipe Costing:** Automatically calculate the cost of goods sold (COGS) for every menu item based on current ingredient costs.

### 3. Waste & Purchasing Module (The "How Much We Waste")

- **Waste Logging:** A simple interface for staff to log spoilage (e.g., "3 lbs of lettuce spoiled due to expiration"). This log directly impacts the inventory count.
- **Usage vs. Purchase Analysis:** Compare what was purchased vs. what was used vs. what was wasted to identify systemic issues (e.g., consistently over-ordering tomatoes).
- **Automated Reordering Suggestions:** Suggest optimal order quantities to minimize waste while ensuring stock levels.
