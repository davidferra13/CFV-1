## PRODUCTION NOTES

- This video overlaps significantly with storyboard #4 (What Your Client Sees) but adds the chef perspective bookending. The key difference: #4 is purely the client experience, #8 shows the round-trip.
- For the split-screen scene (Scene 5), options: (a) record both perspectives separately and composite in ffmpeg post-production, (b) use a CSS split within a single large viewport (3840px wide, each side 1920px), (c) fall back to alternating cuts.
- The activity timeline ("viewed at... accepted at...") is a powerful detail. If this isn't built, simulate it with pre-seeded data showing timestamps.
- The quote line items should be realistic and mouth-watering on the client side (full menu descriptions, not just "dinner - $2,400").
- Record on beta. Two browser contexts for dual perspective.
