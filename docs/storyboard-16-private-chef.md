## The pitch in one sentence

The private chef archetype: solo operator, in-home dining, juggles inquiries, events, clients, recipes, finances, and calendar alone. This video tours the core workflow that replaces their spreadsheet, notebook, calculator, and inbox.

## A-HA MOMENTS (4 total)

1. **Speed:** Inquiry to event in one click. Close-out in 5 steps.
2. **Intelligence:** Client profiles with dietary preferences, kitchen details, and lifetime spend. The chef knows everything before walking in the door.
3. **Money:** Food cost per plate calculated from recipe ingredients. Price with data.
4. **Scale:** Calendar shows the full business: events, prep, travel, blocked days.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- This is the broadest tour video (8 scenes, 90s). Every scene should feel like a glimpse, not a deep dive. Move fast.
- Use the agent account's existing demo data. Need: inquiries with scores, events in multiple states, 2-3 client profiles with history, recipes with costing, calendar with events.
- The close-out wizard should be functional end-to-end. If any step doesn't work, skip that step and show the completed state.
- This video targets the solo private chef. Language is always "you" and "your." No mentions of teams, staff, or stations.
