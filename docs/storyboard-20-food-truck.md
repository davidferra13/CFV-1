## A-HA MOMENTS (3 total)

1. **Speed:** Quick-create an event between orders, from the truck, on a phone. 30 seconds.
2. **Intelligence:** Drive time and leave-by time calculated automatically. Weather included.
3. **Scale:** Week's route with all locations, drive times, setup requirements. One view.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- This video should feel mobile. Consider recording at a phone-sized viewport (390x844 or similar) centered in the 1920x1080 frame with a blurred background or phone mockup frame.
- Alternative: record at full desktop but navigate to mobile-responsive pages that adapt to phone width.
- Travel/route planning is a specific feature. If it's not built as a dedicated page, show the calendar with location notes on each event.
- Drive time calculation may not be built. Show it as text on the event detail ("45 min drive") that looks auto-calculated.
- Packing list checkbox interaction must work on mobile. Test touch targets.
- Quick-create must be accessible on mobile (no Cmd+K on phones, need a + button or FAB).
- Food truck chefs are price-sensitive and skeptical. "Start free" in the CTA is critical.
