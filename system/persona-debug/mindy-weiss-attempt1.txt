# Persona Evaluation: Mindy (The Corporate Event Planner)

**Persona:** Mindy
**Role:** Corporate Event Planner (Manages large-scale, high-stakes corporate events)
**Goals:** Execute flawless, on-time, and under-budget events; maintain strong vendor relationships; minimize last-minute crises.
**Frustrations:** Scope creep; unexpected vendor failures; poor communication between departments; manual data entry across multiple spreadsheets.
**Key Needs:** Centralized communication hub; robust resource allocation tracking; automated compliance/checklist management.

***

## Evaluation Against System Capabilities

**Strengths:**
*   **Project Management:** Excellent for tracking complex, multi-stage projects (e.g., Venue Scouting $\rightarrow$ Vendor Booking $\rightarrow$ Day-Of Execution).
*   **Resource Allocation:** Can track budgets, required equipment (A/V, linens), and personnel needs across different event components.
*   **Communication:** Centralized chat/document sharing keeps all stakeholders (internal teams, external vendors) in one place.

**Weaknesses:**
*   **Niche Focus:** The system is too general. It lacks the specific compliance/safety checklists required for corporate venues (fire marshal sign-offs, insurance verification).
*   **Financial Depth:** While it tracks budgets, it doesn't handle complex Purchase Order (PO) workflows or multi-currency invoicing required for international corporate travel.
*   **Client Relationship Management (CRM):** It treats clients as project stakeholders, not as long-term relationship assets. It lacks historical tracking of client satisfaction scores or preferred vendor lists based on past performance.

***

## Scoring & Recommendations

**Overall Fit Score:** 7/10 (Very Useful, but requires customization for high-stakes corporate environments)

**Recommendations:**
1.  **Integrate Advanced Compliance Modules:** Build out mandatory, role-based checklists (e.g., "Corporate Venue Checklist" requiring uploads of insurance certificates and capacity sign-offs).
2.  **Enhance Financial Workflow:** Implement a formal PO generation and approval workflow that integrates with accounting software (e.g., QuickBooks).
3.  **Develop Client History View:** Create a "Client Profile" dashboard that aggregates all past event data, budget overruns, and satisfaction feedback for quick reference on repeat business.

***

## Summary for Product Team

Mindy needs a **Project Management Suite with deep, customizable Compliance and Financial controls.** The current system is a great *task tracker*, but for Mindy, it needs to function as a *governance and financial control system* to mitigate corporate risk. Focus development on making the "checklist" function mandatory and auditable, rather than optional.
