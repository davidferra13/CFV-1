# Persona Evaluation: Service Lead (High-Touch Operations)

**Persona Profile:** The Service Lead is responsible for the flawless execution of high-end, complex service events. They are the operational backbone, bridging the gap between the kitchen's output and the guest's experience. Their primary concern is *process integrity* and *real-time adaptation*. They require a system that is robust, highly customizable, and capable of handling massive amounts of contextual data simultaneously.

**Key Needs:**
1. **Workflow Orchestration:** Managing multi-stage, sequential processes (e.g., prep $\rightarrow$ service $\rightarrow$ breakdown).
2. **Contextual Data Aggregation:** Pulling together inventory, staffing, guest history, and menu details into one view.
3. **Communication Backbone:** A reliable, structured way to communicate critical, time-sensitive updates across disparate teams (BOH $\leftrightarrow$ FOH).
4. **Custom Reporting:** The ability to build reports that answer "What happened, why, and how can we prevent it?"

**Pain Points:**
* **Siloed Information:** Having to check three different systems (POS, Inventory, Scheduling) to get a full picture.
* **Manual Handoffs:** Relying on verbal handoffs or printed tickets that get lost or misinterpreted.
* **Lack of Scalability:** Systems that work for small cafes but crash or become unusable during a large gala event.

**Ideal Solution Characteristics:**
* **Enterprise-Grade:** Must handle high transaction volumes and complex user roles.
* **API-First:** Must integrate seamlessly with existing, specialized hardware/software.
* **Visual/Process Mapping:** Needs visual workflow builders, not just data entry forms.
