### Primary Failure: Inaccurate Ingredient Tracking

[Describe the ONE biggest operational problem. Not a feature request. A real business pain point. What happens because this problem exists? What does it cost them? 3-5 sentences minimum.]

Every week, I lose an average of $2,000 due to ingredient waste and over-ordering. My team manually tracks ingredients for each event on spreadsheets, which often leads to errors in calculations. This results in either not having enough food or ordering excessive amounts that go to waste after the events. The lack of real-time inventory tracking also causes us to make emergency runs to the store during peak hours, increasing our costs and causing delays.
