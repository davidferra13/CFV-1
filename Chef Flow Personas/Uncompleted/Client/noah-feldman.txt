Client Profile: "Noah Feldman" - Product Manager, Fintech Startup (Conversion-Focused)

If I can’t convert immediately, I churn. It’s that simple. Pricing is the gateway to conversion - if it’s not there, the funnel is broken.

Business Reality
Right now:

I book 4–7 private dinners per year, typically 2–8 guests.

Budget ranges from $1,000 to $5,000. I’m flexible but decisive.

I expect to go from discovery to payment in under 5 minutes.

Dietary needs are minimal but must be captured cleanly.

Locations vary but should not complicate the flow.

I operate entirely through product interfaces - no calls, no emails.

I think in funnels and drop-off points.

Primary Failure: Broken Conversion Funnel
The biggest issue is that pricing is not part of the initial experience. I land on a page, see options, but no numbers. That’s an immediate drop-off. I’m not entering a funnel without knowing the price. Every additional step before pricing increases friction and reduces conversion. If I have to “request a quote,” I’m gone.

Structural Issue: Delayed Value Delivery
The system asks for input before delivering value. I’m entering details without knowing if the offering fits my budget. That’s backwards. Pricing is the first piece of value I need.

Structural Issue: No Instant Commitment Path
Even when pricing appears, there’s often no immediate way to commit. I see a number, but then I have to wait for confirmation or follow another process. That breaks momentum. I want to act while intent is high.

Structural Issue: Inconsistent Pricing Surfaces
Pricing appears in different formats - sometimes per person, sometimes total, sometimes hidden. That inconsistency creates confusion and hesitation.

Psychological Model
I optimize for: speed to decision and frictionless conversion
I refuse to compromise on: seeing price before any commitment
I avoid: multi-step flows that delay pricing visibility
I evaluate tools by: how quickly they move me from interest to payment

Pass / Fail Conditions
For this system to work for me, it must:

Display pricing immediately upon interaction - no gated steps before seeing numbers.

Provide a clear, consistent pricing format (per person + total) at all times.

Allow instant progression from pricing to payment without additional steps.

Maintain pricing consistency across all surfaces - no discrepancies.

Update pricing in real time as inputs change.

Minimize steps between configuration and checkout to reduce drop-off.

Eliminate “request quote” flows entirely in favor of direct conversion.
