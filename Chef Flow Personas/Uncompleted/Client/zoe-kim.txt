Structural Issue: No Explainability Layer
There’s no way to explore “why” behind pricing decisions. I want to drill down into logic.

Structural Issue: Black-Box Adjustments
If pricing changes without explanation, trust is lost.

Structural Issue: Lack of Transparency Controls
I want to choose how much detail I see - high-level or deep.

Psychological Model
I optimize for: understanding and transparency
I refuse to compromise on: explainable systems
I avoid: opaque logic
I evaluate tools by: how well they explain themselves

Pass / Fail Conditions
For this system to work for me, it must:

Provide an explainable breakdown of pricing logic.

Show clear reasons for any pricing changes.

Maintain consistency between explanation and output.

Offer adjustable levels of detail.
