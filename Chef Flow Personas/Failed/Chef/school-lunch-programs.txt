Our current menu planning process is manual and time-consuming, with chefs at each school creating weekly menus based on local ingredient availability. We currently track our inventory using a combination of spreadsheets and verbal communication between schools and the central commissary. This leads to frequent stockouts of essential ingredients at individual schools, causing last-minute substitutions or even meal cancellations. Conversely, we often overbuy by 10-15%, leading to unnecessary waste and increased costs.

The majority of our kitchen equipment is outdated, with many schools still using gas stoves and manual food processors. Finding and retaining qualified kitchen staff is an ongoing challenge for us.
