My biggest operational problem right now is managing batch production efficiently while ensuring meal quality and variety doesn't suffer. Structurally, I'm also struggling with nutritional tracking for each meal. Manually logging every ingredient and calorie count into spreadsheets is time-consuming and error-prone. Psychologically, I prioritize efficiency and ease of use when evaluating new tools. I refuse to compromise on food quality or client satisfaction. 1. Fully integrate with our current inventory management system
2. Automatically generate nutritional labels for each meal 
3. Allow us to set flexible pickup windows for clients
4. Streamline staff scheduling without sacrificing quality control
5. Provide real-time alerts if ingredients are running low
6. Offer a mobile app for clients to customize orders and track deliveries
7. Scale seamlessly as our business grows
