Elena (grazebyelena on social media) is the developer's chef friend and first real external beta tester.

**Business:** Grazing board / charcuterie business. Order-driven (customers order boards for holidays/events). Delivery + pickup model (pickup at "We Feel Good" in Kittery).

**Specs driven by her input:**

1. `chef-opportunity-network.md` - From her voice message about hiring a sous chef + kitchen manager
2. `food-operator-patterns-elena-transcript.md` (research) - From her Easter product story showing social-to-order pipeline

**Key patterns she reveals:**

- Hires through word-of-mouth chef network
- Seasonal/holiday menu rotation (Easter, spring)
- Ingredient sourcing stories as marketing (Austrian cheese, Rustic Bakery crackers)
- A la carte flexibility (full boards OR individual items)
- Social media as primary ordering channel (Instagram stories -> DMs)
- Regional, seasonal business

**Why she matters:** She represents an archetype (artisan food producer) that ChefFlow doesn't explicitly serve yet. Her needs push toward e-commerce/public ordering, which is the biggest gap in the platform.
